The two bugs, addressed in all three

1
Window-control overlay in the status bar

The traffic lights were rendering in a floating strip of their own. In every mockup they sit inside the single unified titlebar — the only place they belong. In SwiftUI: drop the separate top bar and use .toolbar on the window content so the system owns the title area; don't draw a second container behind the lights.

2
The muddy "Add" button

The old button used accent.opacity over a dark sheet, which read as olive. Replaced with a solid gold primary (full-opacity accent, white/ink label, real press state). Fix PrimaryButton to fill with Palette.accent at full opacity and disable via label opacity, not background opacity.
